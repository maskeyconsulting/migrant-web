# Emergency Healthcare Services

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="emergency">Emergency Access</IconHeading>

### Emergency Numbers

- Ambulance: 999
- HMC Emergency: 16060
- Police: 999
- Fire: 999

### Your Rights in Emergencies

All hospitals must treat emergencies regardless of insurance
No payment required for immediate life-saving care
Ambulance service is free

  </ContentSection>

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="medical">When to Seek Emergency Care</IconHeading>

### Go to Emergency For:

- Severe chest pain
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe injuries
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe burns
- Heavy bleeding

### What to Bring:

Qatar ID
Health Card
List of current medications
Emergency contact numbers

  </ContentSection>
