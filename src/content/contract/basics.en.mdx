# Basic Contract Requirements

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="legal">Written Contract Essentials</IconHeading>
  <div className="prose prose-lg">
    ### Required Contract Elements
    - Written in your language
    - Signed by both parties
    - Clear job description
    - Detailed payment terms
    - Working hours specified
    - Leave entitlements listed

    ### What Makes a Contract Valid
    - Both parties must agree
    - Terms must be legal
    - No blank spaces
    - No verbal-only changes

  </div>
</ContentSection>
