<ContentSection>

### Required Documents

<ul>
  <li>Valid Qatar ID</li>
  <li>Passport</li>
  <li>Employment Contract</li>
  <li>Salary Certificate</li>
  <li>Residence Permit</li>
</ul>

### Popular Banks

<ul>
  <li>Qatar National Bank (QNB)</li>
  <li>Commercial Bank</li>
  <li>Doha Bank</li>
  <li>Masraf Al Rayan</li>
</ul>

  <li>Regular password changes</li>
