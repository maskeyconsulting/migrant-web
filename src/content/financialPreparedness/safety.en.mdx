# Financial Safety in Qatar

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="shield">Fraud Prevention</IconHeading>

### Common Financial Scams

<ul>
  <li>Fake job offers requiring payment</li>
  <li>Phishing emails and SMS</li>
  <li>Fake investment schemes</li>
  <li>Counterfeit currency scams</li>
  <li>ATM card skimming</li>
</ul>

### Protection Tips

<ul>
  <li>Never share bank credentials</li>
  <li>Avoid public WiFi for banking</li>
  <li>Check ATMs for suspicious devices</li>
  <li>Keep important documents safe</li>
  <li>Report suspicious activities</li>
</ul>

</ContentSection>
