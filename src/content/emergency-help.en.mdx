<IconHeading icon="passport">Lost Passport or Documents</IconHeading>
<SectionContainer title="" slug="lost-documents">
  If you lose your passport or important documents:
  <ul>
    <li>
      Report immediately to the nearest police station and get a police report
    </li>
    <li>
      Contact the Nepal Embassy for assistance in getting replacement documents
    </li>
    <li>
      Keep digital copies of your important documents in a secure location
    </li>
  </ul>
</SectionContainer>
