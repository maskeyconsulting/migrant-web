<IconHeading icon="passport">Lost Passport or Documents</IconHeading>
<SectionContainer title="" slug="lost-documents">
  If you lose your passport or important documents:
  <ul>
    <li>
      Report immediately to the nearest police station and get a police report
    </li>
    <li>
      Contact the Nepal Embassy for assistance in getting replacement documents
    </li>
    <li>
      Keep digital copies of your important documents in a secure location
    </li>
  </ul>
</SectionContainer>

<IconHeading icon="help">Role of the Nepali Embassy in Qatar</IconHeading>
<SectionContainer title="" slug="nepalembassy">
<ul>
  <li>
    <b>Passport Issuance and Renewal:</b> Assistance with new and renewed
    passports.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Labor Permit Verification:</b> Ensuring labor permits are valid and
    compliant.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Legal Aid and Counseling:</b> Support for legal issues and disputes.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Labor Dispute Mediation:</b> Help resolving conflicts with employers.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Rescue and Repatriation:</b> Assistance for distressed workers and safe
    return to Nepal.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Job Contract Verification:</b> Checking contracts for fairness and
    legality.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Other Consular Services:</b> Power of attorney, birth/death/marriage
    registration, consular ID cards.
  </li>
</ul>
