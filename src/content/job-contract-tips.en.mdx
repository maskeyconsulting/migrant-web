# Understanding Your Employment Contract

![My Job Contract](/jobcontract.png)

<ContentSection>
  <div className="space-y-6">
    <PolicyCard icon="contract" category="Contract Requirements">
      Every worker must have a written employment contract in their own language. 
      This is your legal protection for fair work conditions.
      <MoreInfoButton href="/en/job-contract/basics" />
    </PolicyCard>

    <PolicyCard icon="fee-policy" category="No Hidden Fees">
      The "Free Visa, Free Ticket" policy means you should not pay for:
      - Work visa
      - Flight tickets
      - Recruitment fees (except max NPR 10,000 service fee)
      <MoreInfoButton href="/en/job-contract/fees" />
    </PolicyCard>

    <PolicyCard icon="passport" category="Document Rights">
      You have the right to keep your:
      - Passport
      - Personal documents
      - Contract copy
      - Qatar ID
      <MoreInfoButton href="/en/job-contract/documents" />
    </PolicyCard>

  </div>
</ContentSection>

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="warning-sign">Warning Signs</IconHeading>
  <div className="prose prose-lg">
    Watch out for these illegal practices: - Contract substitution - Illegal
    recruitment fees - Passport retention - Undocumented payments
  </div>
</ContentSection>

<ContentSection>
  <IconHeading icon="checklist">Essential Contract Items</IconHeading>
  <div className="prose prose-lg">
    Your contract must specify: - Job title and duties - Salary and payment
    schedule - Working hours - Overtime rates - Leave entitlement -
    Accommodation details
  </div>
</ContentSection>
